Research

I am a third year PhD student in Meteorology and Physical Oceanography at the Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Science at the University of Miami, advised by Prof. Lisa Beal. My research focuses on estimating the heat transport of the Indian Ocean, using mooring data from the Agulhas System Climate Array (ASCA), along with Argo and satellite data. I am very excited to gain more cruise experience as part of my PhD.

Cruise experience

15 day Agulhas System Climate Array cruise from Cape Town to Port Elizabeth, ZA (2016). Included mooring work, drifter deployment, and CTD casts.

Skills

I have programming experience in Matlab and python, and I am advanced SCUBA certified.
